3.0 INTERNAL PCBA & COMPONENT FUNCTION BREAKDOWN

[U1] ESP32-S3 (EDGE AI MCU)

Acts as the master processing node. Replaces the legacy dual-MCU architecture. Responsible for reading raw I2C data, performing Spectral Normalization (SNV), executing TensorFlow Lite Micro models for regression (Brix %) and classification (Pathogen detection), and managing the BLE 5.0 GATT stack for mobile app sync.

[U2] AS7265x SPECTRAL TRIAD

A highly integrated 18-channel multi-spectral sensor set ranging from 410nm to 940nm (Visible to Near-Infrared). Captures the precise optical signature of reflected photons from the crop tissue. Operates via I2C with a dedicated hardware interrupt (GPIO 10) to signal data readiness to the ESP32.

[U3] SHT40 TEMPERATURE/HUMIDITY

High-accuracy environmental sensor. Spectral readings are highly sensitive to thermal fluctuations. The ESP32 uses SHT40 data to apply real-time thermal compensation algorithms to the AS7265x raw values.

[U4] MAX17043 POWER MANAGEMENT

Monitors the 3.7V Li-Ion battery state-of-charge (SOC) using the ModelGauge algorithm, preventing deep discharge and managing the USB-C charging pipeline.

4.0 OPTICAL CHAMBER CROSS-SECTION ANALYSIS

1. 45°/0° ILLUMINATION GEOMETRY

Broadband LEDs are mounted strictly at a 45-degree angle within the matte black chamber. The AS7265x sensor reads directly at 0-degrees. This CIE standard geometry eliminates specular reflection (surface gloss/glare), ensuring that only the photons that penetrate the tissue and scatter back carrying chemical information are recorded.

2. AMBIENT LIGHT ISOLATION

The soft silicone bellows mechanically conform to the curved surface of fruits or uneven leaves. This creates a pitch-black isolated chamber, ensuring zero interference from the sun's overwhelming infrared spectrum during field testing.

3. PTFE AUTO-CALIBRATION

Before scanning, the mechanical flip-cap remains closed. Inside the cap is a Spectralon/PTFE white reference standard. The device auto-calibrates against this 99% reflective disc to compensate for LED degradation and dark current sensor drift over time.
